Discover the Solution

The Challenge with Manual Evaluation

Time-Intensive Process

Manual evaluation of Detailed Project Reports (DPRs) is slow, causing significant project delays.

Prone to Errors

Human error and varying DPR quality lead to inconsistent assessments and overlooked risks.

Lack of Standardization

DPRs often lack a consistent structure, making fair and efficient comparison nearly impossible.

Our Intelligent Solution

We've developed an AI-based platform that automates the analysis of DPRs. Using Natural Language Processing (NLP) and Machine Learning, our system reads and understands project reports, checks them for completeness, and predicts potential risks like cost overruns or environmental issues. It provides a standardized framework that brings consistency and efficiency to the entire evaluation process.

Key Features of the Platform

Automated Evaluation

Ingests DPRs in PDF/text formats and evaluates them for completeness and compliance.

Inconsistency Detection

Uses NLP to find mismatched budgets, unrealistic timelines, and other inconsistencies.

Risk Prediction

Flags potential issues like environmental impact, resource shortages, or cost overruns.

User-Friendly Dashboard

Presents data and insights clearly for non-technical officials, with multilingual support.

Offline Functionality

Designed to work even in areas with limited or no internet connectivity.

Seamless Integration

Integrates with MDoNER's existing project management systems for a smooth workflow.
