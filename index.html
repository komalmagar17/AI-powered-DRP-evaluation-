Our Intelligent Solution

We've developed a strong AI-based platform that automates the analysis of Detailed Project Reports (DPRs). Using Natural Language Processing (NLP) and Machine Learning, our system reads and understands project reports, checks them for completeness, and predicts potential risks like cost overruns or environmental issues. It provides a standardized framework that brings consistency and efficiency to the entire evaluation process.

Key Features of the Platform

Automated Evaluation

Ingests DPRs in PDF/text formats and evaluates them for completeness and compliance.

Inconsistency Detection

Uses NLP to find mismatched budgets, unrealistic timelines, and other inconsistencies.

Risk Prediction

Flags potential issues like environmental impact, resource shortages, or cost overruns.

User-Friendly Dashboard

Presents data and insights clearly for non-technical officials, with multilingual support.

Offline Functionality

Works even in areas with limited or no internet connectivity.

Seamless Integration

Integrates with existing project management systems for a smooth workflow.
